About the Show

Space news that doesn't talk down to you.

There's no shortage of space content on the internet. Most of it is either too shallow to be interesting or too dense to be accessible. The Space Between lives in the middle — the gap between the headlines and the science behind them.

Every episode, hosts Kolby Van Camp and Dawson Wagner bring the same thing: genuine curiosity, real research, and the conviction that science communication done well changes how people see the world.

Active since mid-2025, the show has built a growing global audience drawn by its combination of accessible science and honest conversation.
