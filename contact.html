Common Questions

Before you reach out

Do you work with clients outside Kansas?

Yes. Consulting and podcast development work remotely. Drone services are on-location, primarily in Kansas and the surrounding region.

How long does a podcast development project take?

Starter packages can be complete in 1–2 weeks. Launch and Professional packages typically run 3–6 weeks depending on scope and your readiness.

What's included in a consulting retainer?

Dedicated hours each month, priority scheduling, and ongoing access via email or text. Retainer clients get continuity we stay inside your project, not just parachuting in for one-offs.

Is the discovery call really free?

Yes. 30 minutes, no pitch deck, no pressure. We want to understand your situation before proposing anything and sometimes the honest answer is we're not the right fit.
