Meet the Team

Built by communicators

Kolby and Dawson bring complementary expertise, a long track record of making things together, and a shared commitment to helping brands build platforms they actually own.

Kolby Van Camp
Founder | Strategy & Communications

Kolby brings broadcast media experience, musical artistry, and elite verbal communication skills to every project. He specializes in helping brands find and articulate their core message and then build the infrastructure to amplify it. He holds a master's degree in mass communications from Kansas State University and serves as sports director in Garden City, KS for Western Kansas Broadcast Center, LLC. He is also founder artistic director for the Sonus Chamber Choir in Topeka, KS.

Dawson Wagner
Co-Founder | Production & Operations

Dawson is the production backbone of Fractal, bringing technical expertise, creative instinct, and a steady presence across all projects. His partnership with Kolby is the foundation the business is built on: complementary strengths and a long track record of making things together. He holds a master's degree in mass communications from Kansas State University and serves as communications faculty at Kansas State University.

Fractal Media Solutions, LLC

Strategic communications and media production for brands ready to build something worth hearing. Based in Kansas.
