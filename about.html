About Us

Built by communicators,
for communicators.

Fractal Media Solutions was founded on one conviction: great communication shouldn't require gatekeepers. We give brands and creators the tools, strategy, and production power to own their platforms.

Our Story

Southwest Kansas roots.
National ambition.

Fractal Media Solutions was launched in Q4 2025 by Kolby Van Camp and Dawson Wagner — two communicators who met as collaborators and became business partners out of a shared belief that the media landscape is fundamentally changing.

The name says it. Fractals are self-similar at every scale — the same structure, whether you zoom in or out. That's how we think about communications: the principles that make a great conversation also make a great podcast, a compelling campaign, and a scalable AI content system.

We're based in Southwest Kansas, but our work reaches nationally and globally through The Space Between — and our AI services clients are anywhere there's an internet connection.

The Team

Two voices. One mission.

🎙️
Kolby Van Camp
Co-Founder · Communications Strategist · Host

Kolby brings a background in broadcast media, music performance, and communications strategy to every project. With elite verbal communication skills and a track record in live performance and production, he specializes in helping brands find and articulate their core message — and then build the infrastructure to amplify it.

He is also a leader in the Kansas arts community, an active choral conductor and arts administrator, and one of the early adopters building serious AI-powered media operations in the region.

🚀
Dawson Wagner
Co-Founder · Producer · Co-Host

Dawson is the production backbone of Fractal Media Solutions — bringing technical know-how, creative instinct, and a steady presence behind the mic and behind the scenes. As co-host of The Space Between, he helps translate complex science into compelling audio content that holds a global audience.

His partnership with Kolby is the foundation the business is built on: complementary strengths, shared standards, and a long track record of making things together.

How We Work

Principles we don't negotiate on

01

Credibility without gatekeepers

We believe the media landscape has shifted permanently. Credibility is earned through consistency and craft — not by having the right credential or the right platform hand you a mic.

02

You own your infrastructure

Any AI system or content engine we build for a client belongs to them — not to a closed platform. We design for ownership, not dependency.

03

Nuance is worth the investment

Attention economies reward volume and outrage. We help clients build an audience that stays — because what they're saying is actually worth hearing.
